💀 Most Devastating Cyber Attacks

🔒 Ransomware

Malicious software that encrypts data and demands cryptocurrency payment.

🚨 Statistics: Attack occurs every 11 seconds globally. Average ransom payment: $1.5M. Prevention: Regular offline backups, employee training.

🎣 Phishing Attacks

Deceptive emails and messages designed to steal credentials and sensitive data.

📧 83% of organizations experienced successful phishing attacks. Always verify sender addresses, enable 2FA, and never click suspicious links.

🌊 DDoS Attacks

Overwhelming servers with traffic to disrupt services and cause downtime.

🛡️ Protection: Use CDN services, implement rate limiting, deploy Web Application Firewalls (WAF), and maintain冗余 bandwidth.

🛡️ Enterprise Security Solutions

🔥 Next-Gen Firewall (NGFW)

Deep packet inspection, application control, and intrusion prevention systems.

🔐 End-to-End Encryption

Protect data at rest and in transit with AES-256 and TLS 1.3 protocols.

💾 3-2-1 Backup Strategy

3 copies of data, 2 different media types, 1 copy stored off-site or in cloud.
